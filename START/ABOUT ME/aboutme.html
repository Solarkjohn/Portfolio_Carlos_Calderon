I am from Ciudad Victoria, Tamaulipas, Mexico. Since I was young, I loved drawing and physical tasks that required assembling or creating. My family taught me the culture of effort, and every day is a testament to that. I am a deeply creative person with an innate passion for graphic and industrial design.

My mind is constantly looking for new perspectives and approaches to tackle creative challenges. My creativity is combined with a high level of organization, allowing me to approach projects in a structured and efficient manner. My ability to make informed and strategic decisions reflects my intelligence in problem-solving. I always strive to reach exceptional standards in my work and bring innovative solutions to every project I am involved in.
